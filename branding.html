Company Goal

Style guide

This company is a local eldritch horror enthusiast group looking to expand their interests to others. This is to bring a better understanding of horrors such as the Cthulhu, the Kraken, and the Gl'bgolyb. This online store is meant to create a friendly outlook for our horrific friends, and to help beginners learn about the vast world of these monsters and have a fun time summoning one for the first time. Our goal is to make things normally available on the dark web a little more friendly and accessible!
